Official Government Partnerships

TitanForge Fitness is proud to be an official partner of the Ministry of Youth and Sports Egypt in implementing nationwide fitness initiatives. Our collaboration focuses on:

  • Developing youth sports programs across all governorates
  • Training coaches and fitness professionals to international standards
  • Creating accessible fitness infrastructure in public spaces
  • Implementing school-based physical education enhancements
  • Promoting women's participation in sports and fitness activities

Our participation in government-led wellness initiatives has reached over 500,000 citizens through various programs since 2022.
